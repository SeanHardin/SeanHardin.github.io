The Witch's House

Description

In this game you play as Viola, a girl who wakes up finding herself in front of a large mansion. A quick walk around shows that she is trapped inside the area so she must explore the mansion to find another way out. The only problem being that the mansion is full of deadly traps that will instantly kill her at a moment's notice.

Personal Experience

> This was the first RPG Maker horror game i had ever played and is also my favorite. There are plenty of save points around the mansion, so even when you do die to the traps, it is never too big of a setback as long as you are saving. The sound effects of the game are pleasant to listen to, and the puzzle design is good as well.

Misao

Description

In this game you play as Aki, who is pulled into another world resembling her school building alongside a number of her classmates. A student who had disappeared 3 months ago has turned into a vengeful spirit and dragged them all there to get revenge. It is Aki's task to explore the school and calm the spirit's anger however she can.

Personal Experience

> In my opinion this was just an average puzzle game. A lot of the deaths in the game are sudden and unavoidable (and frankly, uninteresting), but this isn't so bad because the game includes a hotkey to quicksave at a moment's notice (while disallowing it where it could screw you over). I hate that telephone. Just walking past it kills you, in a 2 space wide passage, so if you aren't thinking about it, there's a 50% chance of sudden death without being prepared....

Mogeko Castle

Description

In this game you play as Yonaka, a high school student who was on her way home to see her brother, but ends up getting spirited away to a kingdom of strange cat-shaped creatures called mogeko. She Hears that she will be able to return home if she reaches a magical door at the top of the castle, so she must climb to the top all the while avoiding mogeko that are hunting her down.

Personal Experience

This one felt more like the creator was just making something stupid for fun, and turned it into a mess of horror and adventure. Also, I should warn that this game depicts scenes that many people would find uncomfortable, so look into it at your own discretion. For the rest of us who can look past that, the adventure of Yonaka to the top is a quirky and enjoyable story to play through. Though the best ending out of all possibilities is not the normal or true end, but actually one of the bad ends, which is just another amusing aspect of this game.
