Rock/Paper/Scissors

The time tested method of settling conflicts since you were a kid

Rules

The rules of Rock Paper Scissors are simple: you choose one of the three hands and your opponent chooses theirs, at which point you show each other at the same time and the winner is determined by whichever player's hand wins against the other. If both hands are the same then its a tie and nobody gets a point.

In short:
Paper beats rock
Rock beats scissors
Scissors beats paper
Nothing wins in a draw
